ProRun

AI-powered running app designed to help users achieve personal goals through structured plans, motivation and intelligent feedback.

Role: UX Designer
Context: Mobile app · B2C · AI-powered product
Activities: UX design · Onboarding · Monetization · Marketing assets

The problem

Many running apps provide metrics and generic plans, but fail to guide users through a meaningful and motivating journey. ProRun needed an experience capable of building trust in the AI, sustaining motivation and balancing usability with monetization.

My contribution

  • Designed the end-to-end UX of the mobile app
  • Defined onboarding and goal-setting flows
  • Designed interactions with the AI running coach
  • Worked on paywall and CTA strategy
  • Ensured consistency between product and marketing assets

Onboarding & setup

Progressive onboarding focused on user goals rather than features.

AI coach & training plan

Conversational AI designed to explain decisions, motivate users and support long-term engagement.

Paywall & monetization

Monetization introduced after value perception, with clear benefits and reduced friction.

Results & learnings

  • Estimated increase in onboarding completion
  • Improved understanding of AI value in early sessions
  • Better balance between user experience and monetization

This project highlighted the importance of trust, clarity and motivation in consumer-facing, AI-driven products.
