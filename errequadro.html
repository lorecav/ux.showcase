ErreQuadro

B2B SaaS platform designed to aggregate and analyze large volumes of complex data. The project focused on clarity, scalability and usability in a data-heavy environment.

Role: UX Designer
Context: B2B · SaaS · Data-heavy product
Scope: UX design · UI design · Design system · Client collaboration

The problem

ErreQuadro is used daily by expert users to analyze structured and aggregated datasets. The main UX challenge was making highly complex tables readable, comparable and operable, without increasing cognitive load or oversimplifying the data.

My role & responsibilities

I worked as the main UX Designer on the project, collaborating directly with the client, developers and stakeholders to translate domain complexity into effective UX solutions.

Design approach

Given the complexity of the domain, the experience was designed prioritizing clarity, efficiency and consistency over visual novelty or minimalism.

High-density data tables

Tables were intentionally designed with high information density to support comparison, scanning and fast decision-making for expert users.

Design system for complex products

A dedicated design system was created to ensure consistency, scalability and predictable interaction patterns across the product.

Contextual glossary

A contextual glossary was designed to support understanding of complex terminology without forcing users to leave their workflow.

System feedback & error handling

Critical flows such as upload and processing were enhanced with clear system feedback, early error detection and actionable messages.

Results & learnings

  • Improved readability and operability of complex datasets
  • Estimated reduction of errors in critical flows
  • Increased user autonomy and confidence

This project reinforced how clarity, consistency and cognitive support are fundamental in complex B2B environments.
