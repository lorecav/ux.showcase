ErreQuadro

B2B SaaS platform designed to aggregate and analyze large volumes of complex data. The project focused on usability, clarity and scalability in a data-heavy environment.

Role: UX Designer
Context: B2B · SaaS · Data-heavy product
Activities: UX design · UI design · Design system · Client collaboration

The problem

ErreQuadro is used daily by expert users to analyze structured and aggregated datasets. The main UX challenge was making highly complex tables readable, comparable and operable, without increasing cognitive load or oversimplifying the data.

My contribution

  • Designed the UX of a data-heavy B2B product
  • Created a dedicated design system focused on tables and system states
  • Defined consistent interaction patterns and feedback mechanisms
  • Worked directly with the client to translate domain complexity into UX solutions
  • Collaborated closely with developers during implementation

Discovery tables

High-density tables designed to support comparison, scanning and fast decision-making.

Document details & glossary

Contextual glossary and overlays used as cognitive support without breaking the workflow.

Results & learnings

  • Improved readability and operability of complex datasets
  • Estimated reduction of errors in critical upload flows
  • Increased user autonomy and reduced need for external documentation

This project reinforced how clarity, consistency and cognitive support are more important than visual polish in complex B2B environments.
