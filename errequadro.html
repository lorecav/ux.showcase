ErreQuadro

Designing clarity in a complex B2B data environment.

Role
UX Designer
Context
B2B · SaaS · Data-heavy product
Scope
UX · UI · Design system · Stakeholder collaboration

The challenge

ErreQuadro is a platform used by expert users to analyze structured datasets daily. The core challenge was supporting high information density without overwhelming users or reducing analytical power.

Approach

Instead of simplifying the product, the design focused on clarity, predictable interaction patterns and cognitive support. Efficiency was prioritized over visual minimalism.

High-density data tables

Tables were intentionally dense to support comparison and expert scanning. Hierarchy, alignment and interaction cues were carefully calibrated.

Design system for scalability

A structured design system ensured consistency across complex modules and reduced friction in product evolution.

Contextual glossary

A contextual glossary allowed users to access terminology definitions without breaking workflow continuity.

System feedback & error handling

Critical flows were redesigned with proactive feedback, actionable error messaging and clearer system states.

Impact

  • Improved readability of complex datasets
  • Reduced friction in high-stakes workflows
  • Increased user autonomy and confidence
